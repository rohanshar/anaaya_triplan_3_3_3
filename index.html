The Solution

"TriPlan 3-3-3 is a triangle-shaped planner that helps us organize after-school time into 3 easy parts:"

3 'Must Do' tasks
3 fun 'Want to Do' joys
3 'Move Time' activities to stay active!

How Each Face Works:

Blue Face - Must Do

Write your 3 most important homework and chores that you MUST complete today

Yellow Face - Want to Do

Plan 3 fun activities you want to enjoy after completing your tasks

Red Face - Move Time

Schedule 3 physical activities to stay healthy and active

Materials: Eco-friendly cardboard, DIY-friendly, and fun to make!
